Fees & Charges

General Payments

Account Holders

The Account holder is responsible for all subscription payments related to their account. Users can create accounts through our dashboard and enroll in courses. Subscription plans may be monthly or yearly, as outlined in our pricing page.

Course Creators

To sell courses on the NUERLO platform, course creators must first link a payment account (Stripe or PayPal) in their account settings and set a price for their course. Course creators are responsible for managing their course pricing and payment settings.

Course Enrollment

Users can enroll in courses through the dashboard. Courses may be published as free ($0) or paid. For paid courses, enrollment requires payment processing through our secure payment gateway.

Platform Commission

NUERLO charges a commission for transactions on paid courses as per our pricing page. Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, or other gateway providers) additionally charge their own processing fees per transaction (typically around 2.9% plus 30 cents per transaction - refer to the respective payment processor's terms and conditions for exact rates).

Taxes

Where applicable, taxes (such as GST, VAT, or sales tax) must be included and indicated by the course creator within the set charge. Users are responsible for collecting and including any other taxes applicable in their given region. For official tax invoices, users must contact the course creator or NUERLO support.

Payment Processing

Currency

Platform charges are in USD, but we may display conversions in other currencies. In some cases, clients may be charged in their local currency. Where USD is the default currency, conversion to other currencies will occur at the current conversion rate, as managed by Stripe, PayPal, or other gateway provider.

Payment Methods

We accept payments through major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods via our integrated payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, and other supported gateways).

Fee Deduction

All platform fees and payment processor fees are deducted from each transaction upon release. Course creators receive their portion of the payment after all applicable fees have been deducted.

Subscription Plans

Monthly and Yearly Plans

Users can subscribe to monthly or yearly plans to access courses and premium features. Subscription fees are charged in advance for the selected billing period. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled by the user.

Cancellation

Users may cancel their subscription at any time through their account dashboard. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial billing periods, except as outlined in our Refund Policy.

Technical Infrastructure

Hosting and Services

The NUERLO platform is hosted on professional cloud infrastructure, including services from Google Cloud, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and other cloud providers. Hosting locations may vary based on service availability and performance optimization.

Data Storage

User account information, course enrollment data, progress tracking, and preferences are stored securely in our database systems. We also use local storage technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage) to enhance user experience and maintain session data.

Additional Services

Technical Support

Basic technical support is included with all accounts. Premium support and additional services may be available at additional cost. For current rates and available services, please visit our pricing page or contact support at support@nuerlo.com.

Custom Development

Custom development, integrations, and specialized services are costed separately. Please contact us at support@nuerlo.com or call (814) 434-6356 to discuss your specific needs.

Important Notes

  • All fees are subject to change. We will notify users of significant fee changes in advance.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they have sufficient funds or valid payment methods on file.
  • Failed payments may result in suspension of account access until payment is resolved.
  • Refunds are handled according to our Refund Policy as outlined in the User Agreement.
  • For questions about fees, charges, or payment processing, please contact us at support@nuerlo.com or call (814) 434-6356.
